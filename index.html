What Can I Do For You?

I have over a decade's worth of experience as a Business Analyst. What does that mean? It means that I know how to work with you to help you identify and understand your current needs, what is and what isn't going right and what the future can look like. I will document your requirements and create wireframes to help you understand how your site or application will look once it's built.

Do you just need a simple website for your business or just to show off your personality? I can help with that. Whether you want a simple one page site or a website that has multiple pages and information, I can work with you to bring your vision to life.

Are your needs bigger than just a website? Do you need an application to help drive your business or engage your users? I can help scope out all the facets of a larger application including database support and API calls to provide your users with the best and most engaging experience possible. Let me help you bring meaningful impact to your users' lives.

My Work

Here are some examples of my most recent projects.

L2 Engineers

L2 Engineers was a single page, continuous scroll website built for a residential engineering firm based in Woodinville, Wa. I worked with the client to understand their business and the tone they wanted to convey. I selected images expressed their vision and quality of work. The site is responsive to mobile and has increased work queries significantly. The tools used were: HTML, CSS, JavaScript and JQuery.

View Site

DEVolunteer

DEVolunteer was a project built as my final project in school. It is a Full Stack application that strives to connect developers and non-profit organizations. The developers volunteer their time and skills to help improve the organizations' online presence or current digital applications. Tools used were: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Angular, MongoDB, Node.js and Express.js.

View Site

Gently Used Pets

Gently Used Pets is a project dear to me. It helps facilitate the adoption of senior and special needs pets. Too often these beautiful animals get overlooked in the shelters. This application allows users to search by pet type, age or special need. It pulls from the PetFinder API and provides full information for each animal, including contact information for the shelter. Tools used were: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery.
